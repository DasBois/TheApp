Ewing Marion Kauffman Foundation

An entrepreneurial spirit is at the heart of the work we do across the Kauffman Foundation every day. Our founder, Mr. Kauffman, believed it was a fundamental right for anyone who had a big idea to be able to bring it to life. We work with entrepreneurs, empower them with tools and resources, and work to break down barriers that stand in the way of starting and growing their businesses. We fight to empower the makers, the doers and the dreamers to create their own destinies and achieve uncommon things.
